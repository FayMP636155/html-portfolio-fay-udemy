About Me

Hi there! I’m Fay Mae Palaña, an inquisitive and analytical thinker with an INTP personality — which means I’m naturally curious, enjoy solving complex problems, and love exploring how things work. I’m also a proud cat person, and I often find myself inspired (and occasionally interrupted) by my feline friends while tinkering with projects or learning something new.

My professional journey has taken me across different roles and countries, giving me unique experiences and perspectives along the way. I’m currently a Student Developer at Uplift Bootcamp, where I build and implement web application features using JavaScript, HTML, and CSS to create dynamic and user-friendly interfaces. Before this, I worked as a Call Center Agent at Teleperformance in Quezon City, assisting customers with empathy and professionalism. That experience strengthened my communication and problem-solving skills in fast-paced, team-oriented environments, and taught me how to monitor and improve service quality.

I’ve also had the chance to work in Australia, Peru, Bolivia, and Brazil across roles such as clothing manufacturing staff, receptionist and bar attendant volunteer, and cashier & fuel attendant. These diverse positions helped me develop strong operational execution and customer service capabilities — all of which are surprisingly useful when thinking about business processes in technical contexts. Living and working internationally also expanded my linguistic abilities: I’m fluent in English, Tagalog, and Portuguese, and I speak Spanish at a B1 level.

I’m passionate about technology and systems thinking, and my goal is to grow into a DevOps role — or whatever that discipline evolves into in the future — where I can combine automation, collaboration, and continuous improvement to help teams build better software faster.

When I’m not working or learning something new, you can find me exploring new hobbies, reading up on tech trends, and hanging out with my daughter and cats.
